What To Expect At Your First Visit

Whether you're new to the area, need a new Fairfax dentist, or you haven't seen a dentist in a while, we're here for you. Our office is a judgment-free zone, and all are welcome. Not sure what to expect from your first visit at our Fairfax dental office? Here's a quick breakdown of the basics:

  • Reviewing dental records — bring them if you have them (optional).
  • X-rays — digital x-rays help us see below the surface.
  • Teeth cleaning — plaque/tartar removal + polish + floss.
  • Oral exam — review x-rays + check teeth/gums.
  • Planning — clear next steps, no pressure.

Why Are Twice-Yearly Dental Cleanings Important?

Cleanings remove plaque and tartar that can’t be eliminated at home. Routine checkups help Dr. Doshi catch issues early—saving time, money, and stress. Six-month checkups are often covered by insurance, and if you don’t have it, our membership plan keeps preventive care affordable.

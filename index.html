What To Expect At Your First Visit

Whether you're new to the area, need a new Fairfax dentist, or you haven't seen a dentist in a while, we're here for you. Our office is a judgment-free zone, and all are welcome. Not sure what to expect from your first visit at our Fairfax dental office? Here's a quick breakdown of the basics:

  • Reviewing dental records - If you have records from previous dentists, you can certainly bring them into our office in Fairfax. This is not required, of course, but having access to your past dental records can help Dr. Doshi and the team at our office provide more informed treatment, and learn more about the oral health challenges that you may have faced in the past.
  • X-rays – For new patients, we recommend a set of digital x-rays. X-rays help our team look below the surface, and identify past dental work, signs of tooth decay and gum disease and identify past dental work, signs of tooth decay and gum disease, and more. X-rays are usually taken before teeth cleanings.
  • Teeth cleaning - Next, one of our friendly dental hygienists in Fairfax will use special tools to scrape plaque and tartar buildup from your teeth. Then, we will polish your teeth and floss to remove any remaining particles.
  • Oral exam - After your cleaning, you’ll get to meet Dr. Doshi. Then, Dr. Doshi will take a look at your x-rays and perform a quick oral exam to check on the overall health of your mouth.
  • Next steps - We’ll talk through your goals, show you what we see, and recommend a plan that fits your priorities, timeline, and budget.
